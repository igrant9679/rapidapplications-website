AFAAVS

Appointments & access validation.

Born as the USAF Forms and Account Management Service (FAMS), AFAAVS automates the appointment and system-access lifecycle for the financial management community — and its pattern generalizes to any component's appointment and access mission.

DD 577 / KS

Appointments & terminations

Appointment workflows for Departmental Accountable Officials and Key Signatories — position management, training prerequisites, waiver artifacts, auto-generated DD 577s, memos, and DAO/KS logs.

DD 2875

System access requests

End-to-end SAAR automation: role-gated approval chains, Block 13 data, automated provisioning into target systems, and generated DD 2875 forms as the official record.

AUDIT

Revalidation & reconciliation

Annual revalidation cycles, account reconciliation against target systems, separation-of-duties rules, and leaver/joiner/mover triggers — directly remediating IT NFR access-control findings.

INTEGRATION

Connected to the enterprise

Integrated with DMDC IWS for personnel data, ADLS and TraX for training records, and CRIS and FM Workflow for automated account provisioning.

KLE

Engagement workflows for headquarters staffs.

Built for SAF/IA's Key Leader Engagement mission: 800+ senior-leader engagements a year, each requiring coordinated preparation across country directors, branch chiefs, and front offices.

INTAKE

Automated request initiation

Engagement requests created from invitations with required fields validated at entry; unique identifiers assigned; the responsible director notified within minutes.

WORKFLOW

Multi-echelon approvals with SLAs

Approval chains from Country Director through Front Office, with fast-track routing for urgent requests, parallel approvals for multi-country engagements, and automatic escalation at SLA thresholds.

DOCUMENTS

Generated staff products

Executive Summaries, Read-Aheads, Country Fact Sheets, and After-Action Reports generated from templates with auto-populated data — version-controlled, with side-by-side comparison.

ANALYTICS

Leadership visibility

Real-time dashboards of active engagements by status, processing time by phase, bottlenecks, and workload distribution — with scheduled reports to leadership.

Result at SAF/IA: preparation time cut from 33 hours to 8.5 per engagement, throughput doubled, and 100% of wet signatures eliminated.
